What would the world be like, once memory could be read, saved and duplicated?
In 2050, the rapid development technology caused extreme disparity between the rich and the poor. Meanwhile, a massive cooporation was engaging in a research, which reveals the technique of sharing, saving and duplicating of the human memory. However, the leader of the research, Dr. Huang, and his assistant, Mr. Chen, are accidentally embroiled into a murder. What was the truth behind the case?
